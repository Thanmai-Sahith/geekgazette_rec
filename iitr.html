Coding Culture

IITR has always been famous for its rich coding culture. There are so many clubs that promote and take this culture forward while cultivating it to become more proficient with time. More than half of the technical clubs promote this coding culture in one way or other, by holding different events like hackathons, workshops, giving out assignments with some highly exhilarating problem sets and many more.

So, are you wondering if these clubs are all about promoting and cultivating the coding culture?
No!, absolutely not! What good is doing all this stuff if it's of no use to us?
These clubs are always working to make our everyday lives easier.
Not only does these clubs do all these things for us(students), but they also do manage many of the institute assets(like the official website, channel i, social media accounts,.....).

The achievements of the students of IITR displayed below are just another indicator of the rich coding culture of IITR.

Achievements

  • Highest no.of GSoC selections among IITs(nearly 40-Second only to IIITH among all the institutes in India).
  • Made it to ICPC world finals and grabbed the first rank among all indian intitues(not once, but twice - 2013, 2015).
  • First position in many global hackathons(like UST global hackathon and such).
  • Second position in CSAW world finals 2019.
  • Papers accepted in top tier conferences such as ICCV, ACMMM, MICCAI, ACPR, ICMI.
